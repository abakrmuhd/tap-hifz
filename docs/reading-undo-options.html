Reading Screen Undo Options

The Reading screen already has Back on the left, Page Bookmark and Settings on the right. These compare how undo can appear after a tap without adding a permanent bottom toolbar.

A. Bottom Undo Snackbar

Appears briefly after logging, then disappears. Standard and hard to miss.
